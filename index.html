Privacy Policy for FlowState

Last Updated: April 2026

FlowState is committed to your privacy.

1. Data Collection

The app does not collect, transmit, or store personal data on external servers.

2. Usage Stats

The app requests Usage Access permission to track time spent in other apps. This data remains on your device and is used only for Focus and Blocker features.

3. Notifications

The app uses the POST_NOTIFICATIONS permission only to provide status updates and warnings related to focus sessions.

4. Third Parties

No data is shared with third parties.

5. Contact

If you have questions, contact: khsupport1@gmail.com
